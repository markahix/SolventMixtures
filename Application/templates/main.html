Solvent Box Maker for Classical MD

This server is designed to help generate solvent boxes with custom molecules. You may generate boxes of any size, but keep in mind larger boxes require more time to generate, while smaller boxes will produce more repetition in the orientation of the individual molecules. It's a balance.
